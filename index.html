About The Band

Heaven on Earth Music

Born out of a collective calling to bring spiritual awakening, **Heaven on Earth Music** is a group of dedicated Christian musicians, singers, and worship leaders. Known for our signature event **Unusual Worship**, we push boundaries to build worship environments that are raw, honest, and filled with power.

We blend contemporary Christian music with traditional gospel roots, incorporating vibrant visual storytelling, energetic performances, and interactive prayer. Our focal objective is simple: create an atmosphere where heaven touches earth, souls are won, and youngsters discover their true identity in Christ.
